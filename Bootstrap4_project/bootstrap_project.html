The Story:

Berserk is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Kentaro Miura.

Set in a medieval Europe-inspired dark fantasy world, the story centers on the characters of Guts, a lone swordsman.

This is a story about revenge and redemption as Guts must fight his way through dmeon hordes in order to protect himself and his friends.
